Vijay Chandrasekhar (born 22 June 1974), known professionally as Vijay, is an Indian politician and actor. In a career spanning over three decades, Vijay acted in 68 films and is one of the most commercially successful actors in Tamil cinema with multiple films amongst the highest-grossing Tamil films of all time and is amongst the highest paid actors in India.
